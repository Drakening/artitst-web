I AM KNOXHILL.

I am not a product of hip hop, I’m just a product of the environment I grew up in. It started off freestyling for money after school, it was fueled by countless losses and late nights trying to find my outlet. I have always been a poet, an artist who loved all types of music. Rap is merely the evolution of Knox Hill. I am a contradiction in every sense of the word. Pro athlete turned musician, backpacker who misses home, introvert who needs a crowd to express himself - welcome to the mind of a man torn by identity. At the center of it all is my one constant in this life: the music that I love. Welcome to my universe.
